# Strategi Marketing - Inter Medi-A E-Commerce Platform

## 1. Digital Marketing Strategy

### A. Search Engine Marketing (SEM)
- **Google Ads Campaign**:
  - Search Ads: Target keywords "printer murah", "service printer Jakarta"
  - Shopping Ads: Product listings dengan gambar dan harga
  - Display Ads: Retargeting visitors dengan visual products
  - YouTube Ads: Video demonstrations dan tutorials

- **Budget Allocation**:
  - Search Ads: 40% (Rp 20 juta/bulan)
  - Shopping Ads: 30% (Rp 15 juta/bulan)
  - Display/Retargeting: 20% (Rp 10 juta/bulan)
  - YouTube Ads: 10% (Rp 5 juta/bulan)

### B. Social Media Marketing
- **Facebook & Instagram**:
  - Organic content: 5 posts/week
  - Paid promotion: Rp 8 juta/bulan
  - Influencer collaboration: Tech reviewers dan business owners
  - Facebook Groups: Join UMKM dan business communities

- **TikTok Marketing**:
  - Short-form content: Product demos, tech tips
  - Hashtag challenges: #PrinterHacks #OfficeSetup
  - Collaboration dengan tech content creators
  - Budget: Rp 3 juta/bulan

- **LinkedIn B2B**:
  - Target corporate clients
  - Share industry insights dan case studies
  - Connect dengan procurement managers
  - Sponsored content untuk decision makers

### C. Content Marketing
- **Blog Strategy**:
  - 2 articles/week: Buying guides, tutorials, industry news
  - SEO-optimized content untuk organic traffic
  - Guest posting di tech blogs dan business websites
  - Email newsletter dengan blog highlights

- **Video Content**:
  - YouTube channel: Product reviews, comparisons, tutorials
  - Instagram Reels: Quick tips dan product highlights
  - Facebook Live: Q&A sessions dan product launches
  - TikTok: Trending content dengan product integration

## 4. Customer Retention Marketing

### A. Loyalty Program
- **Point System**:
  - 1 point = Rp 1,000 spent
  - 1,000 points = Rp 10,000 discount
  - Bonus points untuk reviews dan referrals
  - Birthday month double points

- **Tier Benefits**:
  - Silver (Rp 5 juta): 5% discount, free shipping
  - Gold (Rp 15 juta): 10% discount, priority support
  - Platinum (Rp 50 juta): 15% discount, exclusive products

### B. Email Marketing
- **Automated Campaigns**:
  - Welcome series untuk new customers
  - Abandoned cart recovery (3-email sequence)
  - Post-purchase follow-up dan review requests
  - Win-back campaigns untuk inactive customers

- **Newsletter Strategy**:
  - Weekly newsletter dengan new products
  - Monthly tech tips dan maintenance guides
  - Seasonal promotions dan exclusive offers
  - Customer success stories

### C. Customer Service Excellence
- **Multi-channel Support**:
  - Live chat di website (9 AM - 6 PM)
  - WhatsApp Business untuk quick queries
  - Email support dengan 24-hour response
  - Phone support untuk technical issues

- **Proactive Service**:
  - Order status updates via SMS/email
  - Maintenance reminders untuk service customers
  - Product warranty expiration notifications
  - New product recommendations based on purchase history

## 7. Performance Measurement

### A. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Acquisition Metrics**:
  - Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC): Target < Rp 200,000
  - Conversion Rate: Target 3-5%
  - Traffic Growth: 25% quarterly increase
  - Lead Quality Score: Qualified leads percentage

- **Retention Metrics**:
  - Customer Lifetime Value (CLV): Target > Rp 5,000,000
  - Repeat Purchase Rate: Target 40%
  - Churn Rate: Target < 10% annually
  - Net Promoter Score (NPS): Target > 50

### B. ROI Tracking
- **Channel Performance**:
  - Google Ads ROAS: Target 4:1
  - Social Media ROI: Target 3:1
  - Email Marketing ROI: Target 10:1
  - Organic Traffic Value: Monthly assessment

- **Campaign Analysis**:
  - Cost per acquisition by channel
  - Revenue attribution modeling
  - Customer journey analysis
  - Marketing mix optimization

### C. Reporting & Optimization
- **Weekly Reports**:
  - Campaign performance summary
  - Budget utilization tracking
  - Conversion rate analysis
  - Competitor activity monitoring

- **Monthly Reviews**:
  - ROI analysis by channel
  - Customer feedback compilation
  - Market trend assessment
  - Strategy adjustment recommendations

- **Quarterly Planning**:
  - Budget reallocation based on performance
  - New channel exploration
  - Seasonal campaign preparation
  - Competitive positioning review
