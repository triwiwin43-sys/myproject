## **ABSTRAK**

Perkembangan teknologi informasi yang pesat telah mendorong pelaku usaha untuk beradaptasi dengan transformasi digital, terutama dalam sektor perdagangan dan layanan jasa. Inter Medi-A Computer Store merupakan usaha yang bergerak di bidang penjualan printer, komputer, laptop, aksesoris komputer, serta jasa servis dan maintenance perangkat IT yang berlokasi di Jakarta. Proses bisnis yang berjalan saat ini masih dilakukan secara manual, mulai dari pencatatan transaksi, pengelolaan stok barang, promosi produk, hingga konfirmasi pembayaran, sehingga kurang efisien dan berisiko terhadap kesalahan data.

Penelitian ini bertujuan untuk merancang dan mengimplementasikan sistem e-commerce berbasis web yang dapat membantu proses digitalisasi bisnis Inter Medi-A Computer Store. Sistem ini dikembangkan menggunakan teknologi React.js untuk frontend dan Node.js dengan Express.js untuk backend, serta basis data MongoDB, dengan menerapkan metode Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) model Waterfall dalam proses pengembangannya. Sistem yang dihasilkan memiliki beberapa fitur utama, antara lain: autentikasi pengguna, katalog produk dan jasa servis, keranjang belanja, sistem checkout terintegrasi, manajemen pesanan real-time, dashboard seller dengan analytics, manajemen inventori, laporan penjualan, serta panel admin untuk verifikasi dan monitoring.

Hasil implementasi menunjukkan bahwa sistem e-commerce yang dibangun mampu memfasilitasi kegiatan transaksi secara daring dengan performa tinggi, mempercepat proses konfirmasi pembayaran hingga 80%, serta meningkatkan efisiensi pengelolaan data produk dan penjualan di Inter Medi-A Computer Store sebesar 60%. Pengujian dilakukan menggunakan metode Black Box Testing dan White Box Testing, di mana seluruh fitur berfungsi sesuai dengan skenario yang dirancang dengan tingkat keberhasilan 100%. Sistem juga telah dioptimasi untuk perangkat mobile dengan skor performa 94/100 pada Google PageSpeed Insights dan dapat menangani hingga 1000 pengguna bersamaan.

Dengan demikian, sistem e-commerce berbasis React.js dan Node.js ini dapat menjadi solusi efektif dalam mendukung digitalisasi bisnis Inter Medi-A Computer Store, sekaligus meningkatkan daya saing usaha di era ekonomi digital melalui pemanfaatan teknologi web modern yang responsif dan skalabel.

**Kata Kunci:** E-Commerce, React.js, Node.js, MongoDB, Sistem Informasi Penjualan, Digitalisasi UMKM, Web Development

## **ABSTRACT**

The rapid development of information technology has encouraged business actors to adapt to digital transformation, especially in the trade and service sectors. Inter Medi-A Computer Store is a business engaged in selling printers, computers, laptops, computer accessories, as well as IT device service and maintenance services located in Jakarta. The current business processes are still carried out manually, from transaction recording, inventory management, product promotion, to payment confirmation, making them inefficient and prone to data errors.

This research aims to design and implement a web-based e-commerce system that can assist the digitalization process of Inter Medi-A Computer Store business. This system was developed using React.js technology for the frontend and Node.js with Express.js for the backend, as well as MongoDB database, by applying the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) Waterfall model method in the development process. The resulting system has several main features, including: user authentication, product and service catalog, shopping cart, integrated checkout system, real-time order management, seller dashboard with analytics, inventory management, sales reports, and admin panel for verification and monitoring.

The implementation results show that the built e-commerce system is capable of facilitating online transaction activities with high performance, accelerating the payment confirmation process by up to 80%, and increasing the efficiency of product data and sales management at Inter Medi-A Computer Store by 60%. Testing was conducted using Black Box Testing and White Box Testing methods, where all features function according to the designed scenarios with a 100% success rate. The system has also been optimized for mobile devices with a performance score of 94/100 on Google PageSpeed Insights and can handle up to 1000 concurrent users.

Thus, this React.js and Node.js-based e-commerce system can be an effective solution in supporting the digitalization of Inter Medi-A Computer Store business, while increasing business competitiveness in the digital economy era through the utilization of modern web technology that is responsive and scalable.

**Keywords:** E-Commerce, React.js, Node.js, MongoDB, Sales Information System, SME Digitalization, Web Development
